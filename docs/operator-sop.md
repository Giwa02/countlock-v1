# CountLock V1 Operator SOP

## Purpose

CountLock prevents missing or extra parts from being placed into kits.

## Setup

1. Open CountLock on the phone or tablet.
2. Hold the device above the counting mat — overhead is best, but a steady
   angled hold also works.
3. Confirm the mat is clear.
4. Select the project.
5. Select the kit.

## The gold frame

A gold rectangle is drawn on the camera view. **Only what's inside the gold
frame is counted.** Parts that cross the edge of the frame are treated as
"cut off" and flagged as missing — so always position the camera so every
part for the current group sits fully inside the frame.

## Counting process

For each part group:

1. Place the current group on the mat.
2. Spread the pieces apart so they don't overlap or touch.
3. Compose the shot so **every part is fully inside the gold frame**.
4. Tap **Picture**.
5. Read the detected count and the verdict (PASS / MISMATCH).
6. If the status says one or more parts were cut off at the edge, recompose
   to fit them inside the gold frame and tap Picture again.
7. If the count is wrong, fix the layout and tap **Picture** again.
8. Move that group into the kit.
9. Continue to the next group.

## Buttons

### Back

Moves to the previous part group.

### Picture

Captures the area inside the gold frame and counts visible objects.

### Finished

Locks the kit and sends review results.

## Rules

1. Do not overlap parts.
2. Do not mix different groups.
3. Every part must be fully inside the gold frame at capture.
4. Do not tap Finished until every group has been counted.
5. If the count keeps failing, notify the supervisor.
